Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FocusFlow handles your data.

1. Data Collection

FocusFlow is a client-side application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data (settings, tasks, preferences) is stored locally on your device using your browser's Local Storage.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the YouTube IFrame API to play background music. By using this feature, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. We only use Local Storage to save your timer preferences and task list so they persist between sessions.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact the developer.
